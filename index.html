The Palace of King Mpande

King Mpande was born in 1798 near to present-day Babanango, He was the son of Inkosi Senzangakhona's 9th wife, Songiya ka Ngotsha of the Hlabisa clan. During the reign of his brother King Shaka, he held a position of trust King Shaka presented King Mpande with his first wife, Ngqumbazi, when he was 30 years old, an early age for to marry during the Shakan period.
